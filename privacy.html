CaptionSafe Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-02

CaptionSafe (“we”, “our”, “the app”) helps you prepare photos and videos for social platforms and export them to your device.

Data We Access

  • Photos / Videos (Media Library): If you grant permission, CaptionSafe can access photos and videos from your device so you can preview, edit (e.g., resize, blur background, safe-zone guides), and export results.

How We Use Your Data

  • Media is used only to provide the editing and export features inside the app.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

Storage and Sharing

  • Edited exports are saved to your device (e.g., Photos) when you choose to export.
  • If you use the Share feature, iOS sharing services handle delivery to other apps/services you select.

Subscriptions and Purchases

  • If you subscribe to CaptionSafe Pro, purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive your full payment information (Apple handles billing).
  • We may receive non-identifying subscription status from Apple to unlock Pro features.

Analytics (Optional)

  • CaptionSafe may collect basic, anonymous usage analytics (such as app performance, crashes, and feature usage) to improve reliability and user experience.
  • We do not collect sensitive personal data for advertising.

Data Retention

CaptionSafe does not upload your media to our servers by default. Media stays on your device unless you share it via iOS.

Your Choices

  • You can manage permissions anytime in iOS Settings (Photos access).
  • You can delete exported files by removing them from your Photos app.

Children’s Privacy

CaptionSafe is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: captionsafe@outlook.com
